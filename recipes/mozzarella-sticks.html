Air Fryer Mozzarella Sticks

Description

Need a great appetizer? These cheesy air fried mozzarella sticks couldn't be easierâ€”and since they use the air fryer, the mess is a minimum! Serve with warm marinara or pizza sauce for dipping.

Ingredients

  • 4 egg roll wrappers
  • 4 mozzarella cheese sticks (or string cheese sticks)
  • 1/4 cup water, or as needed
  • cooking spray
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped garlic
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce, or as needed for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Lay an egg roll wrapper on a cutting board, with corners facing up and down, and left and right. Place a cheese stick across the center of wrapper. Roll bottom corner of wrapper up over cheese stick. Fold in left and right corners; seal with a dab of water. Finish rolling up wrapper and seal top corner with a dab of water. Repeat with other cheese sticks and wrappers.
  3. Place the rolls in the basket of the air fryer. Spray lightly with cooking spray. Air fry for 5 minutes. Open the air fryer; turn rolls over. Air fry until golden and crisp, about 5 minutes more..
  4. Meanwhile, stir melted butter, garlic, and parsley together in a small bowl. Remove rolls to a plate and brush with garlic sauce.
